Odomate - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Last Updated: November 22, 2024

General Questions

What is Odomate?

Odomate is a comprehensive vehicle management app that helps you track maintenance, plan trips, and navigate with real-time GPS guidance. It combines AI-powered maintenance recommendations with advanced navigation features.

Is Odomate free?

Yes! Odomate offers a free tier with vehicle management, manual trip tracking, and AI maintenance recommendations. Premium features like GPS navigation, automatic trip tracking, and push notifications require a subscription ($4.99/month or $49.99/year).

What platforms does Odomate support?

Odomate is available for Android and iOS devices.

What languages does Odomate support?

Odomate supports 7 languages: English, Spanish, German, French, Polish, Russian, and Ukrainian.

Do I need an internet connection?

  • Offline: All functionality works offline except navigation, automatic trip tracking, and AI component-life analysis
  • Navigation: Requires internet for real-time routing and map tiles
  • AI features: Requires internet for OpenAI integration

Account & Privacy

How do I create an account?

  1. Download Odomate from Google Play or App Store
  2. Open the app and tap "Register"
  3. Enter your email and create a password
  4. Verify your email address
  5. Start adding vehicles!

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption for: - All data transmission (end-to-end encryption) - Local storage on your device - Cloud backups via Supabase

Do you sell my data?

No. We never sell your personal data to third parties. We collect anonymous analytics to improve the app, but your personal information, trip routes, and vehicle data remain private.

Can I delete my account?

Yes! Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Your data is deleted immediately on the server.

What data do you collect?

We collect: - Email address (for authentication) - Vehicle information (make, model, VIN, maintenance history) - Trip data (distance, duration, routes - stored locally and in Supabase) - Anonymous analytics (device model, OS version, feature usage)

We do NOT collect or share precise GPS coordinates for analytics.

Features & Usage

What's included in the free version?

  • Manage 1 vehicle
  • Manual trip tracking
  • AI maintenance recommendations
  • In-app notifications for maintenance
  • Weather forecast with recommendations for trips
  • Parking mode: timer for paid parking and parking spot finder
  • Vehicle information storage
  • Sync across devices

What's included in Premium?

  • Manage up to 2 vehicles
  • GPS Navigation: Turn-by-turn directions with Valhalla (self-hosted)
  • Automatic Trip Tracking: Background GPS recording
  • Push Notifications: Maintenance reminders
  • Service Book Export: In PDF format
  • Route Planning: Save and share multi-waypoint routes

How does AI maintenance work?

Our AI (powered by OpenAI) analyzes your vehicle specifications (make, model, year, engine type, mileage) and generates personalized maintenance intervals for: - Oil changes - Tire rotations - Brake inspections - Filter replacements - And more!

Can I track multiple vehicles?

Yes! In the free version you can manage 1 vehicle. Premium subscription allows you to manage up to 2 vehicles. Each vehicle has its own maintenance schedule and trip history.

Does automatic trip tracking drain my battery?

We've optimized battery usage, but background GPS tracking does consume more power than normal. Premium users can toggle automatic tracking on/off as needed.

Can I export my service book?

Yes! Premium users can export the service book in PDF format.

Navigation

How accurate is the navigation?

Odomate uses Valhalla (self-hosted) for routing, which rely on Mapbox data. Accuracy depends on map data quality in your region. Always follow traffic signs and laws.

Can I use navigation offline?

No, navigation requires an active internet connection for real-time routing and map tiles.

Does navigation show traffic?

Yes! Premium users can enable traffic visualization in navigation settings.

Can I add multiple waypoints?

Yes! Premium users can create routes with multiple stops and save them for future use.

Why does navigation sometimes reroute me?

Odomate automatically reroutes if: - You deviate from the planned route - Traffic conditions change - A faster route becomes available

How much does Premium cost?

How do I subscribe to Premium?

  1. Open Odomate

  3. Select your plan

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, anytime! Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period. No refunds for partial months.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! New users receive a 3-day free trial of Premium features (if available in your region).

What payment methods do you accept?

Payments are processed through: - Android: Google Play Billing - iOS: App Store In-App Purchase

What happens if I cancel Premium?

  • You keep all your vehicles and trip history

Technical Issues

The app crashes or freezes. What should I do?

  1. Restart the app
  2. Check for app updates in Google Play/App Store
  3. Clear app cache (Settings → Apps → Odomate → Clear Cache)
  4. If the problem persists, contact support@odomate.net with:
  5. Device model
  6. OS version
  7. Description of the issue

GPS location is inaccurate. How can I fix it?

  • Enable "High Accuracy" location mode in device settings
  • Ensure GPS permission is granted to Odomate
  • Use the app outdoors with clear sky view
  • Restart your device
  • Check if other GPS apps work correctly

My trip didn't save. Why?

Trips shorter than 0.1 km (100 meters) are automatically discarded. Also check: - GPS permission is enabled - Internet connection for syncing to Supabase - Sufficient storage space on device

Sync is not working. What should I do?

  • Check your internet connection
  • Log out and log back in
  • Force sync from Profile → Sync Now
  • Contact support if the issue persists

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

  1. On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?"
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Check your email for a reset link
  4. Follow the link to create a new password

Maintenance & Reminders

How do I add a maintenance record?

  1. Select your vehicle
  2. Go to Maintenance tab
  3. Tap "+ Add Maintenance"
  4. Enter details (type, date, mileage, cost, notes)
  5. Save

How do I set up maintenance reminders?

All maintenance reminders are set automatically based on AI-generated schedules. You can edit maintenance intervals for each component as needed.

Can I customize maintenance intervals?

Yes! Edit any maintenance item and adjust the interval (by mileage or time).

Will I get push notifications for maintenance?

Push notifications are only available for Premium users. Enable notifications in device settings and Odomate will remind you when maintenance is due. Free users receive in-app notifications.

Data & Privacy

Where is my data stored?

  • Locally: Encrypted SQLite database on your device
  • Cloud: Supabase servers (North America, Central America, Europe)

Can I use Odomate without cloud sync?

No, Supabase authentication is required. However, all data is cached locally so you can access it offline.

What happens to my data if I delete the app?

  • Local data is deleted immediately
  • Cloud data remains in Supabase until you delete your account
  • Reinstall the app and log in to restore your data

Does Odomate track my location all the time?

No. Location is only accessed during: - Active navigation - Automatic trip tracking (Premium, when enabled) - Saving parking location - Finding parking spot
