Odomate – GDPR Compliance Statement

Date: November 22, 2024

Controller (Art. 4)

Oleksandr Konovalov
Email: support@odomate.net
Website: odomate.net

Purposes of Processing (Art. 5)

  • Provide app functionality (vehicle management, maintenance tracking, navigation, trip logging)
  • Account management and synchronization
  • Support and service notifications
  • Stability, troubleshooting, and security (anonymous analytics/crash reports)
  • AI‑based maintenance recommendations

Legal Bases (Art. 6)

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)): core features, account, sync
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): security, stability, anonymous usage statistics
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) where required: notifications and optional features

Categories of Personal Data

  • Contact: email
  • Usage: interactions, screen views (anonymous)
  • Technical: device/OS, app version, diagnostics/crash data
  • Geolocation: precise/approximate during navigation/trip tracking
  • Vehicle data: make, model, VIN, odometer, service history
  • Trip data: start/finish, distance, route geometry, notes

Recipients (Art. 13)

  • Supabase (authentication/storage)
  • Mapbox (maps/navigation), OSRM (routing), Nominatim (geocoding) – temporary processing of coordinates during active navigation
  • OpenAI (AI recommendations: vehicle/odometer context, without personal identifiers)
  • Firebase (anonymous analytics/crash reporting)

International Transfers (Art. 44 et seq.)

Processing may occur in data centers in North/Central America and the EU. Appropriate safeguards (including Standard Contractual Clauses) are applied to ensure an adequate level of protection.

Retention (Art. 5)

  • Account data: retained while the account is active
  • Deleted accounts: server‑side data is deleted immediately upon account deletion
  • Trip history: retained until you manually delete trips
  • Anonymous analytics/crash data: 26 months (Firebase default)

Data Subject Rights (Art. 12–22)

Right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and portability.
Requests: support@odomate.net.
Complaints: competent supervisory authority (Art. 77).

Security (Art. 32)

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (encrypted databases)
  • Access controls and audit logging
  • Data minimization

Note on Location Data

Precise location is processed only during active navigation or when automatic trip tracking is enabled. No continuous sharing with third parties for advertising/tracking. During navigation, coordinates may be temporarily sent to Mapbox/OSRM/Nominatim for routing.

DPO and Contact

A DPO is not required based on current risk assessment.
