2. Description of Service

Odomate is a vehicle management and navigation application that provides:

2.1 Free Features

  • Vehicle management (add, edit, delete vehicles)
  • Manual trip tracking
  • In-app notifications for maintenance reminders
  • Vehicle information storage
  • Service book export

2.2 Premium Features (Subscription Required)

  • GPS Navigation: Real-time turn-by-turn navigation
  • Automatic Trip Tracking: Automatic logging with background GPS recording
  • Route Planning: Multi-waypoint route creation and saving
  • AI-Powered Maintenance: Personalized maintenance schedules powered by OpenAI
  • Weather Forecast: With driving recommendations
  • Parking Mode: Timer for paid parking and parking spot finder
  • Push Notifications: Maintenance reminders
  • Advanced Analytics: Detailed trip statistics and insights
